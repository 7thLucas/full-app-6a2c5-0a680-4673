The Situation

Why solo creators are stuck

✕
The Problem

Running a solo production means wearing every hat — and most tools only fit one.

  • ▸ Research, writing, design, and organization scattered across a dozen different apps
  • ▸ No single home for movies, games, and creative projects — everything lives in random folders
  • ▸ Visual ideas trapped in your head with no way to share that context with AI tools
  • ▸ Weeks of creative work lost — no way to retrieve what you wrote, built, or directed last session
✓
The Solution

An AI studio that handles the whole production — from first idea to final deliverable.

  • ▸ Intelligent assistant for research, writing, and creative direction in one place
  • ▸ Content creation for trailers, posters, and merchandise — guided by your instruction
  • ▸ Every project organized in its own professional workspace — nothing gets lost
  • ▸ Upload images from phone or PC to give the AI real visual context for your vision
